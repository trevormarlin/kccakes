About Me

Hello, I'm KC Marlin

Welcome to KC's Cakes & Bakes — where every creation starts with your vision and ends with something extraordinary. I'm a passionate cake maker and decorator based right here in Reno, Nevada, turning celebrations into edible works of art.

From intimate birthday cakes to towering wedding masterpieces, I pour creativity, precision, and genuine love into every single layer. Whether you're dreaming of intricate sugar flowers, bold sculpted designs, or timeless elegant tiers — your idea becomes my mission.

Every cake is made from scratch with quality ingredients, baked to order, and designed specifically for you. No two cakes are ever the same.
